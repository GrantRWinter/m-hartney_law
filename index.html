Our Services

This is a home office conveniently located near Centre Street North @ 78th Avenue N.W. with ample parking. We accomodate your schedule and will meet with you on evenings or weekends at your convenience. We are able to accomodate children (including preschoolers) when necessary to make it more convenient for parents. Please advise if you suffer from pet (cat) allergies. If necessary, we will make arrangements to meet at an alternate office or location. We accept VISA, MASTERCARD and INTERAC.

Real Estate and Mortgages  

Whether you are buying, selling or refinancing we will be able to assist you. In a sale by owner, we can help you prepare Purchase and Sale Agreements and take steps to ensure you avoid any pitfalls.


Wills and Estates  

We have extensive experience in assisting Executors with probate applications, including assisting with tax concerns and referrals to other professionals such as Accountants. Our experience extends to dealing with estate matters that have ramifications in other provinces and countries such as the US and UK. We make the procedure as simple and straightforward as possible.

We recommend that everyone have a Will, Enduring Power of Attorney and Personal Directive. We are able to provide them for singles or couples addressing all of your concerns, ensuring that steps will be taken immediately in situations such as accidents or other medical emergencies.

We attend at Hospitals, Residences or Care Facilities to accommodate seniors and shut ins, including on short notice where required.


Commercial/Professional Corporations and Societies  

If you wish to incorporate your business or professional practice (such as medical, dental, veterinary or similar practice) we can assist you as we have many others in the past. We also keep the Corporations and Societies we act for in good standing with the Alberta or Canada Corporate Registries on a yearly basis.


Family Law  

We provide advice and prepare pre-nuptial, cohabitation and separation agreements as well as represent clients in uncontested divorce and separation matters. We represent clients in the Court of Queen's Bench and Provincial Court where required, but our approach is always to reach agreements respectfully and amicably without incurring expensive litigation costs wherever possible.


Notarizations and Parental Travel Consents  

We prepare Notarized Travel Consents for parents allowing children to travel with either parent, relatives or friends as may be required both in and outside of Canada.

We also notarize documents for use both within and outside of Canada, taking special care to ensure that the documents are notarized in a form acceptable in the jurisdiction where they will be used.
